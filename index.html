Parkours

My main student job this year is to help college students to do their homework. I do this thanks to Parkours. It's a company that hires young students that are 18-25 years old. They are divided, in different classes, with other students of the same class and a tutor. There is one tutor by class. I have a classe of secondes this year. This experience teach me many things : I learn everyday how to be more pedagogic. Furthermore, I learn how to make them work, how to make them enjoy working.
